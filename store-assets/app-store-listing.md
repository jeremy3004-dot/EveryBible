## Description (4000 characters max)

Every Bible helps you read, listen to, and grow in Scripture every day.

**READ THE BIBLE OFFLINE**
Read the complete Berean Standard Bible even when you do not have an internet connection. The reading experience is designed to stay clean, focused, and comfortable for long sessions with adjustable font sizes and dark mode support.

**LISTEN ANYWHERE**
Stream or download chapter audio for supported translations and keep listening in the background while you drive, walk, or work. Playback speed controls, sleep timer support, and chapter controls make it easy to stay in the text.

**BUILD A DAILY HABIT**
Track your Bible reading across daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly views. Every Bible helps you pick up where you left off, keep momentum, and stay steady in your reading routine with reminders and activity tracking.

**SAVE WHAT MATTERS**
Highlight verses, add notes, and come back to the passages that stand out. Your saved highlights and annotations make it easier to reflect, revisit, and share what God is teaching you.

**GROW BEYOND READING**
Explore Four Fields discipleship pathways and learning content for personal growth, mentoring, and group study. Every Bible is built for everyday Bible readers who want Scripture close at hand in English, Spanish, Hindi, or Nepali.

Download Every Bible and make Scripture part of your everyday rhythm.
