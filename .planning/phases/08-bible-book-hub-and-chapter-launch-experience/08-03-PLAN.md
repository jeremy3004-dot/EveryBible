# Plan 08-03: Launch Handoff And Preferred Mode Continuity

## Objective

Preserve the user's preferred chapter mode and resume expectations when moving from the browser through the book hub into the shared chapter session.

## Scope

- Add preferred-mode handoff from the hub into the chapter reader.
- Persist the user's chosen chapter launch mode in Bible state.
- Keep the reader session explicit about honoring route-driven preferred mode.

## Out Of Scope

- Queue/history persistence
- Global mini-player mounting
- Companion modules
