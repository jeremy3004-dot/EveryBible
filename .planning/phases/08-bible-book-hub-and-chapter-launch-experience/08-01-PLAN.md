# Plan 08-01: Book Metadata And Fallback Contracts

## Objective

Define a local-first book experience contract that can drive richer book hubs without blocking on a backend or proprietary assets.

## Scope

- Add book-level metadata for synopsis, intro treatment, art direction, and chapter launch context.
- Seed safe fallback content for priority books with explicit availability flags.
- Keep the contract easy to swap to backend-fed data later.

## Out Of Scope

- Saved-library actions
- Companion modules beyond the shared contract shape
- New remote content services
