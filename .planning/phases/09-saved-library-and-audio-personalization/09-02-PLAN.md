## Objective

Expose favorite, share, download, playlist, and library actions from the listen experience and provide one place to revisit saved listening state.

## Scope

- Add an overflow action surface to the reader.
- Build a saved-library screen in the More stack.
- Route saved items back into the shared reader session.

## Out Of Scope

- Global mini-player placement
- Ambient/voice polish beyond honest availability messaging
- Synced or collaborative playlists
