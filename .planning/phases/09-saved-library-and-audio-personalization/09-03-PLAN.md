# Plan 09-03: Mini-Player And Honest Audio Options

## Objective

Keep listening resumable outside the reader with a persistent mini-player and audio option surfaces that describe current catalog limits honestly.

## Scope

- Mount a global mini-player outside the immediate reader screen.
- Extend the audio player hook to coordinate queue playback, resume state, and history recording.
- Add voice and ambient controls that show real availability instead of implying unsupported options.

## Out Of Scope

- Native media session integration
- Multiple narrator catalogs
- Synced playback position across devices
