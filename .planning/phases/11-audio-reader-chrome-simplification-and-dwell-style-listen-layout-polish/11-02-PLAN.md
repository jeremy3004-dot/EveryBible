# Plan 11-02: Rebuild Audio-first Transport And Visual Shell

## Objective

Match the requested Dwell-style listening layout by reducing the transport row to chapter controls plus play/pause and by removing extra nested shells around the audio-first body.

## Scope

- Introduce an audio-first transport variant in `src/components/audio/PlaybackControls.tsx` or a dedicated audio-first transport component if that stays simpler.
- Remove the 10-second back/forward buttons from the audio-first Bible screen only.
- Simplify `src/components/audio/AudioFirstChapterCard.tsx` so the book cover box, title, verse count, progress row, transport, and utility pills are the primary visual elements.
- Remove the background watermark icon and other decorative layers that compete with the main art.
- Keep the current EveryBible palette and current playback hooks.

## Out Of Scope

- Global removal of 10-second skip controls from every player in the app
- Replacing the underlying `useAudioPlayer` contract
- Adding new audio options that do not already exist
