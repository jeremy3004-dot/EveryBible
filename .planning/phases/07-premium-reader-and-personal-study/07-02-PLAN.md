# Plan 07-02: Follow-Along Listen Mode

## Objective

Give listen mode a Dwell-style text action that opens synchronized follow-along reading without interrupting playback.

## Scope

- Add a follow-along text surface reachable directly from listen mode.
- Estimate the active verse from playback progress when exact timing data is unavailable.
- Auto-scroll the follow-along surface to keep the active verse visible.
- Preserve playback when the follow-along surface opens, closes, or the user switches back to the player.

## Out Of Scope

- Exact verse-timing ingestion from remote audio providers
- Queue, history, or playlist surfaces
- Cross-screen mini-player behavior
