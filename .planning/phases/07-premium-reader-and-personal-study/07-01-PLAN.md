# Plan 07-01: Shared Chapter Session Shell

## Objective

Turn the Bible reader into one chapter session that can move between `Listen` and `Read` without losing chapter, verse focus, or playback context.

## Scope

- Add a pure chapter-session model that decides the active mode and mode transitions.
- Introduce a segmented `Listen / Read` shell in the chapter screen when audio is available.
- Keep chapter navigation, translation selection, and playback state anchored to the same screen session.
- Preserve audio-first fallback behavior for chapters that do not have text.

## Out Of Scope

- Follow-along text overlay details
- New saved-library actions
- Book hub redesign
