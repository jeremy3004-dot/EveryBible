Runtime note from the live pilot:

  - `--s3-no-check-bucket`
  - `--s3-disable-checksum`
  - `--header-upload "Cache-Control: ..."`
- `s5cmd` should stay disabled for this rollout unless its R2 multipart behavior is revalidated.
