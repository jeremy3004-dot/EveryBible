# Plan 10-02: Reusable Companion Sections

## Objective

Render the new companion modules through reusable section and card components attached to the book hub.

## Scope

- Add shared section and card components for passages, devotionals, figures, plans, and playlists.
- Mount the sections beneath the core book hub content.
- Preserve graceful empty-state behavior for books with little or no seeded content.

## Out Of Scope

- Dedicated deep-detail screens for every companion module type
- Remote fetching
- Personalized ranking of companion items
