# Plan 10-03: Analytics And Coverage For Companion Flows

## Objective

Add a thin analytics seam and verification coverage around book-hub and companion interactions so the new modular surfaces stay observable and testable.

## Scope

- Add a lightweight analytics service for hub, companion, and library interactions.
- Track launches, empty-state exposure, and core saved-library actions without introducing a vendor dependency.
- Verify the new model and store behaviors through targeted tests plus broad regression runs.

## Out Of Scope

- Real analytics backend ingestion
- Experiment framework rollout
- Dashboard/reporting work
