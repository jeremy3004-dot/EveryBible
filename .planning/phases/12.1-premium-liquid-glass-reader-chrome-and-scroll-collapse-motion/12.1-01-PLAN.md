# Plan 12.1-01: Define The Premium Reader Motion Model

## Objective

Capture the supplied video as an explicit motion contract so the premium reader redesign has deterministic thresholds instead of ad hoc animation guesses.

## Scope

- Add pure reader-motion helpers and constants in `src/screens/bible/bibleReaderModel.ts`.
- Add failing tests first for scroll-progress clamping and collapse thresholds in `src/screens/bible/bibleReaderModel.test.ts`.
- Add source-level assertions for the required overlay architecture in `src/screens/bible/bibleReaderChromeSource.test.ts`.

## Out Of Scope

- Final styling of the glass surfaces
- Full `BibleReaderScreen` layout rebuild
- Release packaging
